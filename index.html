Typography

We typeset this page in Proxima Nova, designed by by Mark Simonson Studio, and Chaparral Pro, from Adobe’s Carol Twombly. We also utilize Vernon Adams’s Pacifico script in our logo. All typography are served care of Typekit.com.

Technology

FriendlyDesign.co is laid out using Jeet.gs by MOJO Tech and styled with SASS. Fancy typing animation courtesy of Matt Boldt's Typed.JS. And of couse none of this would be possible without Stack Overflow.

Team-ology

We built FriendlyDesign.co on a growing collection of MacBook Pros, using the Adobe Creative Suite v5.0, with the help of Firebug, Chrome, and a host of friends and colleagues. We fueled this undertaking with MadCap Coffee provided through Canvas.Co/Work, at which we are among the founding members.
